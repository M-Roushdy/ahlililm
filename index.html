A Few Words About Us

Dar Al-Ilm School has a tremendous history since its founding in 1952 by Dr. Muhammad Kamal Suleiman under the name “Manial Al-Rawda Educational School”, under the slogan (originality/development/excellence).
Since the establishment of the school, successive generations have graduated for years with the ability to bear responsibility in all fields to help the nation.
Dr. Muhammad Kamal Suleiman had an unparalleled interest in the educational aspect, as he believed that education was the first step to building the nation, and he was interested in choosing the best distinguished teachers.
In addition, we believe in the psychological aspects, that help the students develop their social skills and increase self-confidence.
With the passage of years and the development of the school to keep pace with the times and changes, interest in all aspects of children increased, and the school was developed with the latest technologies to deliver information in the best form.
